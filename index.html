RIDE FOR LIFE

PREGNANT WOMEN IN RURAL AREAS WILL GET IN TOUCH WITH THE NEAREST DRIVERS TO PROVIDE A SAFE RIDE FOR THESE MOTHERS .

About us

Ride For Life is a transportation system that serves mothers in Uganda by connecting mothers with drivers. Drivers will use a motocycle ambulance to serve the community especially mothers.

How it works

Drivers will receive a notification from the nearest rider that is requesting a ride if they set on their profile are avaible. Riders will be able to see if any drivers are available in their location .
